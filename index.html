About Us

MMTC is a leading maid agency based in Singapore, specializing in providing trained helpers from our own training centre in Yangon, Myanmar. We have been serving satisfied customers since 2021.

We offer a wide range of domestic helper placement services, including full-time helpers, transportation services, passport renewal services etc.
We provide ongoing support to both our customers and helpers, ensuring a smooth and successful placement experience.
Our training centre in Yangon provides comprehensive training programs for domestic helpers, ensuring that they are equipped with the necessary skills and knowledge.

Our Own Training Centre

Since 2022, our Training Centre in Yangon, Myanmar, has been committed to providing highly skilled and reliable helpers.
Our Fresh Myanmar Helpers are rigorously selected and undergo a mandatory two-month training program covering infant care, elderly care, household chores, and cooking.
Language courses in English is also included to ensure effective communication in diverse households. Our Training Centre demonstrates our dedication to excellence, enhancing our helpers' capabilities to exceed household expectations.
